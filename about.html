ABOUT DRUGS

Alcohol

Type: Depressant

Effects: Relaxation, lowered inhibitions, poor coordination

Risks: Liver damage, addiction, accidents, withdrawal can be dangerous


Cannabis

Type: Psychoactive / Depressant / Hallucinogen (mild)

Effects: Euphoria, altered perception, appetite increase

Risks: Memory issues, anxiety, dependence with heavy use


Hallucinogens

Type: Hallucinogen

Effects: Altered reality, visual/auditory hallucinations

Risks: Bad trips, psychosis, anxiety, flashbacks


MDMA

Type: Stimulant + Hallucinogen

Effects: Euphoria, empathy, high energy

Risks: Dehydration, serotonin syndrome, long-term brain effects


Heroin

Type: Opioid

Effects: Pain relief, intense euphoria, sedation

Risks: High addiction risk, overdose, withdrawal, respiratory depression


Benzodiazepines

Type: Depressant (sedative)

Effects: Anxiety relief, muscle relaxation, sleep aid

Risks: Dependence, withdrawal seizures, overdose (especially with alcohol)


Cocaine

Type: Stimulant

Effects: Intense energy, alertness, confidence

Risks: Heart attack, paranoia, addiction, nasal damage


Ketamine

Type: Dissociative anesthetic

Effects: Detachment, hallucinations, pain relief

Risks: Memory loss, bladder damage, dependency


Prescription Opioids

Type: Opioid

Effects: Pain relief, sedation, euphoria

Risks: High overdose risk, especially fentanyl; addiction
