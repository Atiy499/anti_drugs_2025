Success Story

Ali ka Naya Safar:

Ali ek middle-class family se tha, Karachi ka rehne wala. Uski zindagi bilkul normal chal rahi thi jab tak wo college gaya. Wahan uski dosti kuch aise logon se hui jo drugs use karte thay — pehle cigarette, phir weed, aur dheere dheere wo heroin tak chala gaya. Shuru mein sab “time pass” lagta tha. Lekin dheere dheere, drugs uski zindagi ka hissa ban gayi. Ghar walon se jhoot, paise churaana, dost chhor dena — ye sab roz ka routine ban gaya. Teen saal tak wo addiction mein doobta gaya.


Girne ka Lamha:

Ek din Ali overdose le betha. Hospital le jaaya gaya — uski jaan mushkil se bachi. Ye moment uske parents ke liye bhi eye-opener tha. Unho ne faisla kiya ke Ali ko rehab bhejna hoga.


Rehab ka Safar:

Rehab mein pehle dino mein Ali ne bohot struggle kiya. Withdrawal symptoms, depression, guilt — sab kuch tha. Lekin wahan usne ek counselor se mulaqat ki jo khud pehle addict reh chuka tha. Usne kaha: "Agar mein nikal sakta hoon, tu bhi nikal sakta hai. Par tujhe sach mein chahna padega." Ali ne us din se sach mein koshish shuru ki. Therapy, group sessions, meditation, exercise — sab kuch adopt kiya.


Nayi Zindagi:

Aaj Ali ko clean hue 6 saal ho chuke hain. Wo ab ek motivational speaker hai, jo school aur colleges mein lectures deta hai addiction ke khilaf. Usne apni life ka mission bana liya hai ke wo aur logon ko is andheray se nikaale.
