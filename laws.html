DRUGS LAWS IN PAKISTAN

What Is Illegal?

Drug Use: Illegal to consume any narcotic or psychotropic substance.
Possession: Even small amounts of drugs like cannabis, heroin, or ice are illegal.
Sale & Trafficking: Highly punishable with severe jail time or death penalty in large cases.

Special Cases

Minors: Involving children or selling near schools leads to harsher punishment.
Cannabis (Charas, Bhang): Illegal but sometimes tolerated culturally. Still punishable.
Prescription Drugs: Illegal to use or sell without a proper prescription.

Enforcement & Rehabilitation

Anti-Narcotics Force (ANF): Main agency handling drug law enforcement in Pakistan.
Rehabilitation: Courts may order rehab for addicts, but availability is limited.

FAQs & Common Myths

Is cannabis legal in Pakistan? No. All forms of cannabis are illegal, even if commonly used.
Can first-time users go to jail? Yes, even small possession is a crime.
Can a person get rehab instead of jail? Sometimes, especially if the person is an addict and not a trafficker.
